DragonFire Rules

1. Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, stalking, sexism, racism, lies, obscenities, false information and incitement to hatred are strictly prohibited on the server.

2. It is forbidden to send spam or engage in self-promotion (invitations to the server, advertising, etc.) without the permission of one of the administrators. This rule also applies to private messages from server members.

3. It is forbidden to post explicit and obscene content. This rule applies to texts, images, avatars or links containing or describing scenes of sex, nudity, scenes of violence or other shocking content.

4.If you notice someone who breaks the rules, or unpleasant content, contact the server administration. We want the server to be safe for everyone!

5. You cannot put nicknames that cannot be pinged, contain bad words or that simply cannot be written on a regular keyboard. In this case, we will change your nickname.

6. You cannot be inadequate (for example, a lot of spam, massive violation of the rules (especially rule 1), a lot of pings)

7.Do not violate the Discord Terms of Service (But you can break the rule that better discord cannot be used) and Discord Safety Center
